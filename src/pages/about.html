About Digital Summit 2020

Your opportunity to engage and be inspired

We understand life is busy, and we want to you to get the most out of the Summit.

This is your opportunity to network, engage and be inspired to transform and adapt in a changing digital landscape.

We want to provide you with a packed event experience. Our program includes:

  • keynote presentations each day
  • panel discussions
  • virtual exhibition
  • workshops
  • case studies
  • on-demand presentations

You'll get the tools and inspiration you need to push forward your digital transformation journey.

You will:

  • hear from digital practitioners and thought leaders
  • virtually engage with digital practitioners and peers and build your networks
  • ask questions and present problems to our industry and government exhibitors
  • learn how to begin your digital transformation journey
  • engage in workshops, case study discussions and thought-provoking presentations
  • celebrate the best examples of digital practice across government and industry working with government.

Digital transformation is a team sport

Digital transformation happens when we work and learn together. We invite digital leaders and practitioners working at every level of government, and industry working with government, to the Digital Summit.

If you are a member of the Digital Professional Stream, the Digital Summit is a great opportunity for you to build capability and networks across government and industry.

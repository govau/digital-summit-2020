Acknowledgement and communication

We will communicate with all registered attendees by email. We will send you a confirmation letter and tax invoice when you have completed your registration and payment through the registration portal. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder before contacting the Digital Transformation Agency (DTA).

If you change your email address after you have registered, please email the change to summit@dta.gov.au.

Payment of fees

You must pay for your registration when you register. We do not reserve any registration types.

Transferring your ticket(s)

You can transfer your ticket to someone else if you cannot attend the Digital Summit after registering.

Send your transfer request to summit@dta.gov.au. We accept transfers until 5:00 pm, Friday 23 October 2020.

Cancellations and refunds

  • We accept cancellations until 5:00 pm, Friday 23 October 2020.
  • We will provide a refund until this time with an administration fee of $99.00.
  • You will receive your remaining refund within 14 business days of the cancellation request.
  • We will pay this into the account you used to pay for the tickets.

We will not refund cancellations after 5:00 pm, Friday 23 October. Exceptions to this are at the discretion of the Digital Transformation Agency.

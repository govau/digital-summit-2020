A virtual experience

We've engaged an innovative system to deliver the experience you would expect at an in-person event. It's online, interactive, and virtual.

You can take part in live panel discussions, set up one-on-one meetings with other attendees, and explore virtual exhibition areas. You can network virtually, exchange contacts details with other attendees, and watch keynotes from internationally renowned presenters.

Digital Summit 2020 is a celebration of all things digital. It will allow attendees, sponsors, exhibitors, and presenters to meet in a secure space to adapt to, engage with, and deliver digital transformation.

Event features

  • The Timeline is your live agenda of sessions and activities on one easy-to-use screen.
  • Attend sessions virtually, including keynotes, presentations, panel discussions, workshops, case studies and on-demand content.
  • The Meeting Hub is our virtual meeting place for one-on-one space interactions with other attendees to network, swap contact details and exchange ideas.

Engage with your peers

You can engage with presenters and other attendees during the event. Participate in question-and-answer sessions with the presenter, take notes and discuss presentations with other attendees on the Discussion Forum.

Virtual exhibition

You can meet with our sponsors and exhibitors as you would at an in-person event. Explore the virtual exhibition area, browse the booths and meet our exhibitors.

Live support

Having difficulties? Chat or share your screen with one of our support team to get technical assistance during the Digital Summit 2020.

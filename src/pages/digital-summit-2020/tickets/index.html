Workshops

Close 11:59 pm, 8 November 2020.

We will announce the workshops soon. Register to get updates.

  • Workshop registration is $165.00 per workshop.
  • Each ticket is for a single workshop only.
  • Workshops limited to 30 attendees.
  • You cannot transfer tickets to another workshop.
  • There are 2 workshops on each day.
  • Each workshop is 55 minutes long.

Day 1 – Disruption and Change

12:30 pm to 1:30 pm, Tuesday 10 November

  • Experimental futures: designing a prosperous nation, Lana Thorpe and Francois Kirsten (Deloitte Digital)
  • 55 minutes in the digital ethics pressure cooker, Darren Menachemson (Thinkplace)

Day 2 – Human-centred Services

12:30 pm to 1:30 pm, Thursday 12 November

  • Solve for one, extend to many: a practical hypothetical exercise in inclusive design for people with disabilities, Zoe Rose (Synergy)
  • Digital design and disadvantaged clients: bringing voices at the margins to the centre of digital service design and delivery, Jane Cipants and Judith Levitan (Legal Aid NSW)

Day 3 – Leadership and People

12:30 pm to 1:30 pm, Tuesday 17 November

  • Agility accelerator, Julian Smith (DTA) and Craig Smith (SoftED)
  • The team retro: reflecting on working from home, Anthony McGiness and Saakshi Joshi (Future Friendly)

Day 4 – Technology and
Data

12:30 pm to 1:30 pm, Thursday 19 November

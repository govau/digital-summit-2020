APS and partner agencies

Dear colleagues,

We invite digital leaders, practitioners, and professionals to register and attend the Digital Summit 2020.

The Digital Transformation Agency will deliver the Digital Summit across 4 days between 10 and 19 November. The event will be completely virtual.

The Digital Summit will bring together digital thought leaders, visionaries and practitioners from government and industry. You will hear how some of the biggest organisations in the world are delivering digital transformation and connect with a diverse range of digital leaders.

The Australian Government Digital Awards are part of the Digital Summit program this year, recognising and celebrating the digital achievements and innovations across Australian government and industry.

The Digital Summit program is designed around 4 themes:

  • Disruption and Change
  • Human-centred Services
  • Leadership and People
  • Technology and Data

There will be keynote presentations from the Hon Stuart Robert MP, Minister for the National Disability Insurance Scheme and Minister for Government Services, Rebecca Skinner, CEO of Services Australia, and Peter Woolcott AO, the Australian Public Service Commissioner.

You can view the full program and register here.

Join the Digital Summit 2020

Adapt. Engage. Deliver.

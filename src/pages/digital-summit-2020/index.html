On-demand registration

We're now offering on-demand registration. If you missed out on the Digital Summit, you can still get access to our content.

On-demand registration includes:

  • welcome sessions
  • government keynotes
  • plenary sessions
  • award presentations
  • case study videos
  • Virtual Exhibition access
  • more presentations from government and industry leaders.

Please note on-demand registration does not include:

  • workshops
  • case study discussion
  • other live portal features such as live support, Meeting Hub or Virtual Exhibition booth meetings.

Prices

  • Standard registration for all days is $150.
  • Single-day registration is $50.

All prices include GST and are in Australian dollars.

Adapt. Engage. Deliver.

Adapt

Adapt to and navigate change in your workplace. Our speakers, presenters and workshop facilitators are driving real change and transformation in their organisations to adapt to our changing world.

Engage

Engage with and learn from our speakers and your peers. Challenge how you think and explore new ways to understand user needs and build solutions.

Deliver

Deliver solutions to real users. Build networks and develop practical actions and plans. Get insights into how some of the biggest organisations are delivering digital transformation, from success stories to valuable failures.

Australian Government Digital Awards

We presented the finalists and winners on:

  • Tuesday 10 November – Outstanding digital product or service awards
  • Thursday 12 November – Excellence in partnerships awards

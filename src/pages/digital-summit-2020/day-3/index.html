Digital Summit 2020 Day 3

Day 3: Leadership and People

Tuesday 17 November 2020

Leadership and people play an essential role in digital transformation. Digital leadership can transform how government provides services.

Emerging and recognised leaders will share their stories and inspire us to work smarter and lead better:

  • open leadership
  • ethical leadership and transparent decision-making
  • the future of work
  • workplace innovation
  • agile government leadership.

Why you should attend

  • Understand and make the best of the digital transformation opportunities change creates.
  • Get inspiration and hear from experts who initiate and harness change.
  • Learn how to lead and manage through change and disruption.

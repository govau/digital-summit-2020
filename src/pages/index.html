View the program

This year, you can join the transformation conversation from the comfort of your own screen. Our program has been carefully crafted to provide you a similar in-person experience, online.

Can't make it? Register and you'll be able to play it back on demand for up to 12 months.

We want you to bring your passion for digital transformation and connect with government and industry from across Australia. We want you to share your ideas, knowledge and experiences and tell your story of digital transformation.

Adapt. Engage. Deliver.

Adapt

Adapt to and navigate change in your workplace. Our speakers, presenters and workshop facilitators are driving real change and transformation in their organisations to adapt to our changing world.

Engage

Engage with and learn from our speakers and your peers. Challenge how you think and explore new ways to understand user needs and build solutions.

Deliver

Deliver solutions to real users. Build networks and develop practical actions and plans. Get insights into how some of the biggest organisations are delivering digital transformation, from success stories to valuable failures.

Your opportunity to engage and be inspired

Network, engage and be inspired to transform and adapt in a changing digital landscape.

We're providing a packed event experience. Our program includes:

  • keynote presentations each day
  • panel discussions
  • virtual exhibition
  • workshops
  • case studies
  • on-demand presentations

You'll get the tools and inspiration you need to push forward your digital transformation journey.

You will:

  • hear from digital practitioners and thought leaders
  • virtually engage with digital practitioners and peers and build your networks
  • ask questions and present problems to our industry and government exhibitors
  • learn how to begin your digital transformation journey
  • engage in workshops, case study discussions and thought-provoking presentations
  • celebrate the best examples of digital practice across government and industry working with government.

Digital transformation is a team sport

Digital transformation happens when we work and learn together. If you are a digital leader or practitioner at any level of government, or working with government, you are welcome at the Digital Summit.

If you are a member of the Digital Professional Stream, it is a great opportunity for you to build capability and networks across government and industry.

Audience

We invite digital leaders and practitioners or those interested in digital in federal, state and territory, or local government, and industry to attend.

We recognise that digital transformation works best when we collaborate and learn together.

Speakers

We have a full program of local, national, and international speakers. They are thought leaders in government, industry, and academia.

Recordings

We will record all sessions.

Everyone who registers will have access to the recordings no more than 2 business days after the Digital Summit. These recordings will be available for 12 months after the Digital Summit.

About the Digital Transformation Agency

The Digital Transformation Agency (DTA) hosts the Digital Summit and Australian Government Digital Awards.

We help government improve digital services to make them simple, clear, and fast. We're committed to improving people's experience of government services. This means improving the skills within government to deliver these services.

Visit our website for more information about who we are and what we do.

Microsoft enables digital transformation for the era of an intelligent cloud and an intelligent edge. Headquartered in Sydney, Microsoft Australia has 7 regional offices across the country employing over 1,000 people. Woven throughout daily life, work or play, Microsoft technologies enable millions of Australian partners and customers to innovate in new ways. Our platforms harmonise the interests of our customers, developers, and IT better than any competing ecosystem or platform. Microsoft is driven to help anyone make the most of any moment – to help create a world where people can make the most of their time and to lead more fulfilling lives.

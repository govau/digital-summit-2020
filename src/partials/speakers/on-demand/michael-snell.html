Airservices Optimal Reality — digital twin of the skies

The aviation industry is entering the most disruptive decade since the first powered flight. Airservices Australia is investing to be ready.

Deloitte Digital and Airservices Australia developed a digital twin of Australian airspace that ingests live flight data and simulates forward faster than real time. It simulates thousands of possible outcomes every minute, optimising to the best one. Initial results indicate significant operational benefits and a transformation in how they innovate and deliver for the future.

Bringing Formula 1 thinking, human-centred design, and cutting-edge technology the team created an accurate, performant digital twin and a range of customer-centric apps to improve decision-making across the industry.

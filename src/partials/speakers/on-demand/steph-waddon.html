The future of work is here

We will provide an overview of trends in the future of work, and how they could impact digital transformation within the public sector. The discussion will include an overview of the key workforce disruptors, the impacts of automation, social and demographic change, shifts in resourcing models and structures, and demand for different skills. We will use which our PwC case study examples to demonstrate these.

We will present a pragmatic approach to tackling future of work challenges and provide insights on our methodology.

Clayton Smith

Clayton is a user experience and content design specialist. He has consulted on digital transformation projects for a number of Australian Federal Government Agencies. This experience served him well with his work on the Department of Christmas Affairs website, where he delighted in the challenge of spreading some Christmas cheer through government-style content.

Clayton is co-presenter for 'Checking it twice: digital transformation of the naughty and nice list with the Department of Christmas Affairs'.

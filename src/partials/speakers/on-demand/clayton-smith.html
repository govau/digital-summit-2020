Clayton Smith

On-demand

Clayton is a senior user experience and content design consultant at NTT. He has worked on digital transformation projects for the Department of Health, the Department of Agriculture, the Department of Veterans' Affairs, and the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission. He also teaches service design at the University of Canberra.

Clayton is co-presenter for 'Checking it twice: digital transformation of the naughty and nice list with the Department of Christmas Affairs'.

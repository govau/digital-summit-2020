Executive General Manager – Transformation & Enablement, Australia Post

John is EGM Transformation and Enablement Australia Post. He is responsible for evolving Australia Post's Transformation Strategy in the face of sustainability pressures and a need to rapidly pivot operations due to COVID-19. His career has covered the financial services such as banking, payments, insurance, wealth and markets, retail, government services and logistics.

Customer transformation – putting the customer at the heart of Australia Post's digital transformation

Australia Post has seen unprecedented parcel volumes throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, and experienced significant disruption across our operational network. They have relied heavily on technology solutions and real-time data insights to quickly identify and respond to challenges to make sure they continue to meet the needs of their customers and the community.

The pandemic has reinforced the position that the customer experience must be at the heart of Australia Post's transformation. They strive to create simple and flexible experiences which delight their customers, and this principle is shaping their future network design and technology landscape.

Cybersecurity — increasing cyber resilience above and beyond administrative compliance in 2020

The session will cover a range of highly relevant security topics for government departments. This includes the importance of overarching secure cloud and the successful implementation of risk mitigation strategies and focus on the issues and complexities involved with successfully passing audits and delivering projects and transformations of this nature. The session will also more deeply go beyond becoming merely administratively compliant and focus on increasing overall cyber resilience.

A key outcome will be the ability to determine the level of maturity a department is at and increasing visibility into their own cyber security posture and the strategies involved in developing a roadmap for achieving the desired security posture. This approach to remediation involves leveraging the ecosystem of people, processes and emerging technologies to gain compliance, confidence and resilience along with fusing security into the entire department operating model.

As a partner of the Federal Government's Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) and a member of the Defence Industry Security Program (DISP), ASG Group is provided with continuous threat intelligence which allows us to stay continuously at the leading edge of current security programs and best practices.

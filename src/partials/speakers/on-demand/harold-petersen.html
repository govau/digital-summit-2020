Devops, Lean IT and agile: theory is one thing ... but what about the people?

This presentation introduces how the establishment of an organisational target capability is a holistic combination of multiple dimensions, and how people are a critical enabler. It demonstrates how leadership needs to drive the holistic alignment across all dimensions. It provides inspiration, insights and ideas on some ways to uplift people's capabilities around agile, lean and devops, and online collaboration.

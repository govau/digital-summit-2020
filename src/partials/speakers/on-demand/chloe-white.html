Chloe White

On-demand

Department of Industry, Science, Energy and Resources

Chloe is the Australian Government's National Blockchain Roadmap Lead, responsible for driving the day-to-day implementation of Australia's first whole-of-government blockchain policy strategy. Before this, she was the lead blockchain policy advisor at the Treasury where she led the review into initial coin offerings, advised on blockchain in finance, and was the Department's standing representative at the Council of Financial Regulators DLT Working Group. She has completed postgraduate studies in public policy at the United Nations University and holds an undergraduate degree in economics and finance from UNSW.

Blockchain: does it deserve a second chance?

Chloe will be busting common myths and misconceptions about blockchain and provide examples of how blockchain has become more efficient, private and value-adding since the peak of blockchain's hype cycle around 2017 and 2018.

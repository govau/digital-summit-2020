Assistant Secretary, Communications, Department of Prime Minister and Cabinet

Dana is the Assistant Secretary of Communications in the Department of Prime Minister and Cabinet. She manages a team working across digital communication, media, strategic, corporate, and internal communications, speechwriting, and audio-visual production. She was closely involved in establishing Australian Government communication and whole-of-government coordination during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Australia.gov.au — COVID-19 crisis response

On 17 March, there were 453 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Australia — more than double what it had been only 3 days earlier. With a national emergency on the horizon, the Australian Government needed a communications channel to deliver clear and consistent messages.

The DTA worked closely with PM&C to deliver the communications platform, with Deloitte Digital as our primary partner. With a team of over 40 people working remotely, we leveraged rapid prototyping design methodologies and strong cross-agency collaboration to build and launch a new australia.gov.au COVID-19 crisis homepage in under 6 hours.

Ben Neal

On-demand

PwC

Ben is a Fellow of the Australian Human Resources Institute and the UK-based Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development. He holds postgraduate HR and business qualifications with more than 20 years' experience working in government. Before joining PwC, he led the corporate and HR functions at the Department of Prime Minister and Cabinet and the Department of Immigration.

The future of work is here

Ben and Steph will provide an overview of trends in the future of work, and how they could impact digital transformation within the public sector. The discussion will include an overview of the key workforce disruptors, the impacts of automation, social and demographic change, shifts in resourcing models and structures, and demand for different skills. They will use their PwC case study examples to demonstrate these.

They will present a pragmatic approach to tackling future of work challenges and provide insights on their methodology.

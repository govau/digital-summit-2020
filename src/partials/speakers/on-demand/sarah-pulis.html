Intopia

Accessibility considerations for ICT procurement — ensuring staff and citizens can use the systems government buys, adapts or builds

Sarah Pulis is Director and Co-Founder of Intopia with 10 years of experience working in accessibility. Sarah founded Intopia 4 years ago after working with PwC Australia and Media Access Australia. Sarah also participates in the Education Outreach Working Group of the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative. She is the founder of A11y Bytes and A11Y Camp, which have grown to be Australia's largest events on digital accessibility and inclusion.

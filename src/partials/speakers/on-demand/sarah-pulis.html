Sarah Pulis

On-demand

Intopia

Sarah is Director and Co-Founder of Intopia and can't compete with Andrew's years of experience (10 years isn't bad though, right?). She founded Intopia 4 years ago after working with PwC Australia and Media Access Australia. She also participates in the Education Outreach Working Group of the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative. She is the founder of A11y Bytes and A11Y Camp, which have grown to be Australia's largest events on digital accessibility and inclusion.

Sarah is co-presenter for 'Accessibility considerations for ICT procurement – ensuring staff and citizens can use the systems government buys, adapts or builds'.

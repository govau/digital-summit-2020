Ed Bridgeland

Workshop

Senior Client Partner, Publicis Sapient

Ed leads the Government Human Services practice at Publicis Sapient in Australia. He has worked in and for the public sector for over 15 years, starting his career in Central Government in the UK. He is passionate about improving how governments deliver services to citizens relying on them, and has helped Federal, State and Local Governments to enhance customer services, maintain sustainable costs and ultimately achieve better outcomes. He has successfully worked across many areas of Government service, though is particularly focused on the human and social aspects of the sector, including welfare, justice, and community services.

Ed is co-presenter for 'Designing a better ecosystem for data and digital services'.

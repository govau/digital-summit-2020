Judith Levitan

Workshop

Manager, Client Service, Legal Aid NSW

Judith has worked in the public legal assistance sector for over 15 years. Previously she was a lawyer in a private practice. She has degrees in law and social work. In her latest role she brings the voice of the client into the strategic conversation about how Legal Aid NSW designs and delivers services.

Judith is co-presenter for 'Digital design and disadvantaged clients: bringing voices at the margins to the centre of digital service design and delivery'.

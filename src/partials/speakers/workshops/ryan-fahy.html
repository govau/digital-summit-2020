Ryan Fahy

Workshop

Senior Executive Partner, Gartner

Ryan joined Gartner in October 2019 as a Senior Executive Partner. He advises a range of clients in the financial services, resources, infrastructure, health sectors, and all levels of government. He brings a strong business perspective to this role, linking business change to technology. He has experience interacting with Boards for ASX-listed companies. Before joining Gartner, he was the Chief Information Officer for Healius and Woolworths, where his team delivered the technology solution for its new customer rewards program. He also spent 12 years working for the Commonwealth Bank and as a strategy consultant with Boston Consulting Group. In a previous life he served as an officer in the United States Navy where he operated nuclear reactors on ships.

Ryan is a co-presenter for 'SASE: Cloud-first architecture'.

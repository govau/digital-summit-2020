Jane Headon

Workshop

Director, Strategy & Data, Publicis Sapient

Jane leads Publicis Sapient's Data and Analytics practice. She is a strategic thinker with a track record helping clients design customer and citizen strategies and implementing data-driven marketing. Her work has covered marketing, communications and customer experience capabilities – both business and technical, covering content and communications, channel strategy, audience strategy, analytics, data and decisioning, and the voice of the customer. She brings an engaged and relevant voice to global and local issues around data regulation, privacy and ethics. For this workshop, she will bring her knowledge of global trends in data practices and technology to the agile governance domain, providing strategic foresight to underpin a vibrant and provocative conversation.

Designing a better ecosystem for data and digital services

In this workshop, Jane and Ed explore the challenge of 'How do we build a better ecosystem for digital service delivery and trusted data flow across borders – both local and global'.

For government, we're currently experiencing a watershed moment, signalling the criticality of fast-tracking digitisation of citizen services. But there continues to be many barriers to why, how, when we share data for digital service delivery. Join this interactive workshop to hear an overview of major global trends and innovations in the regulatory, privacy and ethics data landscape.

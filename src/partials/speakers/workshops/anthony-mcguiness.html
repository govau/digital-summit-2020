The team retro: reflecting on working from home

There is no playbook for what we are experiencing. Our teams have had to adapt to a new normal overnight. At Mentally Friendly, we have been running regular team retros since the pandemic began. This has enabled us to adapt and adjust as a team and promoted transparency and trust among leadership and staff. We have charted team sentiment moving from anxiety in uncertainty, to enjoying the silver linings of working from home, to video call fatigue and a desire to reconnect in person.

We want to share the benefits of the team retro. The session will draw on lessons documented in our remote team guide https://acceleration.team/, and encourage participants to engage in an abridged live team retro, individually carding up and playing back their own reflections on what has and hasn't worked well for them.

Julian Smith

Workshop

Agile Lead, Digital Transformation Agency (DTA)

Julian Smith is the Head of the Agile practice at the DTA and is a certified trainer, coach, and practitioner. He has worked in digital ICT for over 20 years, including roles in project and product management, agile and project management offices, digital strategy, enterprise architecture, and user experience. He is passionate about public sector agility and the benefits it provides agencies in Australia and the world. He was a member of the DTA's first UX working group and other committees. Today he works with agencies, helping to build capability in digital ways of working He has an interest in strategic agility, adaptive portfolio management and enabling digital culture change.

Agility accelerator

Today 'agile' is no longer just a buzzword. From building spacecraft to manufacturing, some of the most complex and largest organisations in the world are using agile ways of working to deliver better outcomes, respond to change, improve quality, foster more productive and happier teams, and reduce risk.

This hands-on and interactive session is aimed at helping public sector organisations build capability to support agile ways of working, from policy development through to service design and delivery.

Participants will have access to resources and materials to help accelerate capability building within their own organisations.

SASE: Cloud-first architecture

COVID-19 has significantly increased remote work, potentially changing some of the assumptions behind perimeter-based security. Organisations are increasingly looking to secure their critical applications and data through the uses of Secure Access Service Edge (SASE) technology. This session will:

  • Challenge the thinking that organisations can be adequately protected by treating the edge as an impenetrable barrier.
  • Provide participants with an understanding of Secure Access Service Edge (SASE), and its benefits.
  • Discuss a workable roadmap to achieve SASE given the current state of technology in many government organisations.

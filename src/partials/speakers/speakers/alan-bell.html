Alan Bell

Speaker

Executive Director, Digital Identity at Department of Internal Affairs, New Zealand

Alan is an experienced strategist in government digital in New Zealand, and a senior leader in the office of the Government Chief Digital Officer. His previous roles include leading the development of New Zealand's first Cyber Security Strategy, developing the government's cloud computing policy and policy lead for New Zealand's ICT Strategy. He is currently leading a NZ Digital Identity Transition Program.

Alan is a panel member for 'A global perspective on Digital Identity.'

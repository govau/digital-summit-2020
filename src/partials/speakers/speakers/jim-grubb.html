Jim Grubb

Speaker

Chief Technology Evangelist, Cisco

As an expert in digitisation even before it was called digitisation, Jim has spent most of his career helping people understand how business and technology intersect. He is an exponential thinker and charismatic speaker currently working with most of Cisco's largest customers as they build their digital strategies. As former Cisco CEO John Chambers' long-time product-demo sidekick, he delivered over 400 keynote demonstrations at industry, technology, and CXO events, including Cisco Live, CES, HIMSS, NRF, Oracle World, Salesforce, DODIS, Cisco Partner Summit, and Cisco's World-wide Sales meetings. He has also presented to many Government and World Leaders, including Hillary Clinton, George W. Bush, Tony Blair, Madeleine Albright, Dimitry Medvedev, Al Gore, Viktor Chernomyrdin, Vicente Fox, Arnold Schwarzenegger, Jean Carnahan, and Jim Gilmore. When he's not explaining technology, he is an avid astrophotographer, musician, pilot, and boater.

Living in a digital world

Since the arrival of the vacuum tube, technology has significantly impacted how work gets done. Today's innovations continue to accelerate the pace of change. Simply, we are living in a digital world.

Jim explores:

  • how current technologies such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, robotics, virtual and augmented reality, voice and haptic user interfaces are making us more capable
  • how the Internet-of-Things and blockchain are combining with new levels of connectivity, including WiFi6, 5G, and low-Earth orbit satellite systems to allow us to re-invent the way we work.

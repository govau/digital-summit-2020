Fiona Hathaway

Speaker

Managing Director, Global People Team, Slalom

Fiona Hathaway is the Managing Director for the Global People Team at Slalom Consulting. Her international career spanned Asia, Europe, and the US, and in HR in IT companies like KPMG and PWC. In her global HR Director role at Microsoft, she drove several large-scale global business transformations. Her current challenges are working to drive global expansion into new markets while implementing modern work practices and ways of working which will support high paced growth through digitalisation and innovation.

Culture eats strategy for breakfast

Culture must act as a strong foundation and enabler to any workplace transformation we are looking to accomplish, from digital transformation through to our move to new ways of working. When your strategy changes the quickest thing to derail you if you don't pay attention to it is your culture, your culture will be what defines and drives the collective actions of your workforce. The ability to evolve your culture could be the single indicator which is the best measure of your organisations future success. A successful cultural shift unlocks the potential for innovation, positive disruption of market models and brings new opportunities, but it requires agility and an ability for your workforce to be adaptive not reactive.

The session will discuss in a practical way some learnings of how make a cultural change to come to life and how to live it in a way that adds incremental value or meaning to your organization and your customers.

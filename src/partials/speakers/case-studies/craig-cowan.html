Craig Cowan

Case study

Prouct Manager – COVIDSafe, Digital Transformation Agency

Craig is the Product Manager for the DTA's COVID-19 Digital Responses including the COVIDSafe App, Coronavirus Australia App and the Australian Government's COVID-19 WhatsApp channel. Craig is passionate about delivering user-focussed products using agile methods and making sure product management is effective in a government context. Before his current role, he was the Product Manager for the Digital Marketplace. The Digital Marketplace has awarded over $2.5b in contracts with a strong focus on helping small and medium enterprises to gain government work.

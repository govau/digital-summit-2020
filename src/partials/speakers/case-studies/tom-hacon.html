Tom Hacon

Workshop

Hypothesis

Tom works at the intersection of physical and digital, blending originality with persistence to deliver valuable experiences to his customers. He believes in having passion, autonomy, mastery, and purpose. He is currently Principal Product Manager at Service New South Wales.

How one team rapidly delivered drought, bushfire and COVID-19 crisis support to NSW citizens

Faced with the drought, bushfires, and COVID-19, the NSW State Government needed to rapidly support affected citizens.

Tom will speak to how a persistent, cross-functional team in Service NSW were able to understand what citizens really needed and come back to home base to deliver it in weeks, rather than months. The case study is backed by how the work happened, including:

  • human-centred design in the field, not the desk
  • the practice, not theory, of lean and agile development
  • pairing to build capability in Service NSW using Extreme Programming.

Tom works at the intersection of physical and digital, blending originality with persistence to deliver valuable experiences to his customers. He believes in having passion, autonomy, mastery, and purpose. He is currently Principal Product Owner at Service New South Wales.

How one team rapidly delivered drought, bushfire and COVID-19 crisis support to NSW citizens

Faced with the drought, bushfires, and COVID-19, the NSW State Government needed to rapidly support affected citizens.

This case study talks to how a persistent cross functional team in Service NSW were able to really understand what citizens really needed and come back to home base to deliver it in weeks (not months). The case study is backed by how the work happened, calling out specifically:

  • Human-centred design in the field not from the desk
  • Lean-agile development – practice not theory
  • Extreme Programming – pairing to build capability in Service NSW

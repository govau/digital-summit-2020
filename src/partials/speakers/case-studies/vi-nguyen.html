Vi Nguyen

Case Study

KPMG

Vi has over 17 years' experience as a software engineer, digital strategist and innovator at Macquarie Bank, Volvo Cars, Swedish Space Association, and start-ups. She has led high-profile digital transformations and designing future labs in Australia, Asia and Sweden. She has been described as an 'entrepreneur at heart, with a heart'.

Designing services: a Swedish perspective

After spending 3 years within service and business design in Sweden, Vi's presentation will provide an insight into how service design is applied in Sweden, how this differs from Australia and what we could possibly adopt to fast-track designing for a human-centred government. Vi would like to impart some of her observations, while they are still fresh, to start the discussion, and possibly generate new ideas.

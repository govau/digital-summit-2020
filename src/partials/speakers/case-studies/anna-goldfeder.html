Anna Goldfeder

Case Study

Cogniss

Anna leads the end-to-end product design, development, and implementation of the Cogniss platform. Before she joined Cogniss in 2016, she spent 7 years designing, building and managing award-winning educational games, assessment platforms and core curriculum resources with leading secondary publisher Jacaranda (Wiley).

Anna is co-presenter for 'How human transformation technology is empowering governments and service providers to deliver rapid mobile mental health responses during COVID-19'.

Portable

Simon is co-founder of Portable, a design company based in Melbourne. Portable responds to social need and policy failure and puts users in the centre. He has led the company's work in legal innovation since 2012. He is a Fellow at the Sir Zelman Cowan Centre at Victoria University, and a Teaching Fellow at Melbourne Law Scool teaching in Design for Innovation.

amica.gov.au — a digital solution for separating couples

Portable and National Legal Aid have recently launched amica.gov.au - a tool to help couples separate without the need for a lawyer. It leverages co-design, intuitive content and artificial intelligence to help people resolve their dispute themselves. This has resulted from 3 years user testing and agile development. Since being launched by the Federal Attorney-General on June 30 2020, over 1000 people have started this process, with agreements already being made between parties. Amica is an initiative of Portable that aligns with its mission to provide government services that meet and exceed the expectations of citizens.

From overwhelmed phone lines to SA Health's first virtual employee. How we built virtual agent Zoe in 6 days to respond to COVID-19

Designed by Clevertar and SA Health's team, we built and launched Zoe in 6 days to help SA Health respond to a surge in COVID-19-related questions to the state's hospital switchboards and contact lines.

At a time when demand for health-related information has increased exponentially, Zoe is working around-the-clock to answer questions and help South Australians follow official health guidelines.

The COVID-19 situation is changing rapidly with new information coming to light on a daily basis. Since its launch, Clevertar has been able to re-train Zoe and deploy new conversations in a matter of days.

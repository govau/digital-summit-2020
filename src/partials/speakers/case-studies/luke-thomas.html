Portable

Luke is Senior Legal Designer at Portable, working across legal design and technology products. He led the user research for Amica and worked to simplify the existing government processes as well as supported the data team on creating machine learning model to provide guidance for separating couples. He holds a Juris Doctor from Melbourne Law School.

Luke is co-presenter for 'amica.gov.au – a digital solution for separating couples'.

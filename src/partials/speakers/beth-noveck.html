Professor Beth Simone Noveck

Director, The Governance Lab

Beth is an author, professor and White House advisor, the go-to expert on fusing innovation with leadership at the highest level. She is a pioneer in helping institutions evolve into more productive forms and specialises in employing digital technology to make government more effective at solving problems, delivering services and developing policies. She offers a picture of the revolution in governance unfolding around us, providing insight drawn from her experience working with governments, innovators, and everyday people.
